# RepairCoin App - Tester Installation Guide

This guide explains how to install the RepairCoin staging app for testing purposes.

---

## iOS (TestFlight)

### Prerequisites
- iPhone or iPad running iOS 15.1 or later
- Apple ID

### Installation Steps

#### Step 1: Check Your Email
1. Look for an email invitation from **App Store Connect** or **TestFlight**
2. The email subject will be something like: *"You've been invited to test RepairCoin"*
3. **Important:** You must accept this invitation before you can install the app

#### Step 2: Accept the Invitation
1. Open the invitation email on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap **"View in TestFlight"** or **"Start Testing"**
3. If prompted, sign in with your Apple ID

#### Step 3: Install TestFlight (if not already installed)
1. Open the **App Store**
2. Search for **"TestFlight"**
3. Download and install the TestFlight app (it's free, made by Apple)

#### Step 4: Install RepairCoin
1. Open the **TestFlight** app
2. You should see **RepairCoin** in your available apps
3. Tap **"Install"**
4. Wait for the app to download and install
5. Once installed, tap **"Open"** to launch RepairCoin

#### Troubleshooting
- **Don't see the invitation?** Check your spam/junk folder
- **Invitation expired?** Contact the development team for a new invite
- **Can't find app in TestFlight?** Make sure you accepted the email invitation first

## Android (Play Store Internal Testing)

### Prerequisites
- Android phone running Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
- Google Play Store installed
- Google account

### Installation Steps

#### Step 1: Enable Developer Mode in Play Store

1. Open the **Google Play Store** app
2. Tap your **profile icon** at the top right corner
3. Scroll down and tap **"Settings"**
4. Tap **"About"**
5. Find **"Play Store version"** at the bottom
6. **Tap "Play Store version" 7 times rapidly**
7. You should see a message: *"Developer mode is now enabled"*

#### Step 2: Enable Internal App Sharing

1. Go back to Play Store **Settings**
2. Tap **"General"**
3. Scroll down and tap **"Developer options"** (this appears after enabling developer mode)
4. Find **"Internal app sharing"** and **turn it ON**
5. Confirm if prompted

#### Step 3: Install the App

1. You will receive a **download URL** from the development team
2. Open the URL on your Android device (tap the link or paste it in your browser)
3. The link will open in Play Store
4. Tap **"Download"** or **"Install"**
5. Wait for the app to download and install
6. Once installed, tap **"Open"** to launch RepairCoin

#### Troubleshooting
- **"App not available"?** Make sure Internal App Sharing is enabled (Step 2)
- **Developer options not showing?** Make sure you tapped Play Store version 7 times (Step 1)
- **Download not starting?** Try clearing Play Store cache: Settings > Apps > Play Store > Clear Cache

## After Installation

Once the app is installed:

1. **Open RepairCoin**
2. **Allow notifications** when prompted (recommended for testing)
3. **Sign in** with your test account credentials
4. Start testing and report any issues to the development team

- OS version (e.g., iOS 17.2, Android 14)
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots or screen recordings if possible
