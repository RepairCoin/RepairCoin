# How do I issue an RCN reward to a customer?

Send RCN tokens to a customer's wallet after a completed repair so they
can use them on future visits.

## When to do this

Right after a repair or service is completed — issuing rewards promptly
keeps customers in the loop and drives repeat visits.

## Steps

1. From the shop dashboard, open the **Issue Rewards** tab in the left
   sidebar.
2. In the **Customer Name or Wallet Address** field, enter either:
   - The customer's wallet address (starts with `0x…`), OR
   - Their registered name — the field will look them up.
3. Enter the **repair amount** in USD. The reward amount in RCN is
   calculated for you based on the repair amount and the customer's
   tier (Bronze / Silver / Gold).
4. Review the calculated reward.
5. Click the issue button to send the RCN to the customer's wallet.

The customer's wallet balance updates immediately and they get a
notification of the new reward.

## Common pitfalls

- **The issue button is greyed out.** Your shop needs an active
  subscription or 10,000+ RCG to issue rewards. See
  [subscription-and-billing.md](subscription-and-billing.md).
- **"Cannot Issue to Your Own Wallet."** RepairCoin blocks shops from
  sending rewards to their own wallet to prevent self-dealing. Use a
  different wallet address.
- **Insufficient RCN balance.** You can only issue rewards from RCN
  you've already purchased. If your balance is low, top up first via
  the **Purchase** tab — see [purchase-rcn.md](purchase-rcn.md).
- **Tier bonus surprised the customer.** Silver and Gold customers
  earn bonus RCN on top of the base reward (+2 RCN Silver, +5 RCN
  Gold). The calculation includes those automatically; you don't add
  them manually.
