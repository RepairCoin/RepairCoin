1 Platform Health Copilot — expanded intelligence Shipped

The admin assistant that answers plain-English questions about the whole platform gained three new capabilities.

  • Shop churn-risk — surfaces active shops that have gone quiet (no activity over a window), ranked most-stale first, so the team can re-engage before they leave.
  • Top-performing shops — ranks shops by actual RCN purchase revenue over any window.
  • New-signup trends — reports new shop & customer growth.

The copilot now answers across six platform tools (overview, token economy, subscriptions, churn, top shops, growth). Commit 2b654ff5.

2 Daily Executive Briefing — with UI Shipped

An automated "state of the platform" digest leadership sees the moment they open the copilot.

  • A "Today's Briefing" card with a headline plus bullets on growth, token-economy flow, subscription health, and what needs attention.
  • Cost-engineered: generated once per day and cached (one AI call/day regardless of viewers), with a one-click regenerate.
  • Resilient: falls back to a clean data-only summary if AI is briefly unavailable, so it always renders.

Commit 1eab9ce2.

3 Trust & Safety — scan controls Shipped

Made fraud detection both automatic and controllable for admins.

  • "Run scan now" button — administrators can trigger the detection engine on demand (and verify it).
  • Run-on-deploy — the scan now also runs shortly after each deployment, not just on a 24-hour timer, so findings stay current even on frequently-restarted infrastructure.
  • Scanning is now automatic + scheduled + on-demand.
