Engineering Update

Finishing the Admin Gap Analysis — 3 More Tools

Announcements, referral & marketplace analytics, and coalition-token oversight — the last of the hidden-capability gaps.

Date:  July 7, 2026 Status:  ✓ Delivered & Pushed to main Commits:  5 shipped

Summary

We closed out the admin gap analysis with three more capabilities — an Announcement broadcast composer, a Referral Analytics dashboard, and an Affiliate Groups oversight view — plus surfaced a fully-built Marketplace Analytics screen that had never been mounted, and removed another obsolete component. Together with yesterday's four tools, the admin dashboard now exposes every significant backend capability that was previously invisible.

1 Announcement Broadcast NEW

The notification delivery system (in-app, real-time, and native push) existed, but there was no way for an admin to send a platform announcement. There's now an Announcements composer:

  • Audience selector — Shops, Customers, or Everyone, each showing a live recipient count.
  • Title + message with character limits, and a confirmation preview before sending.
  • Fans out across all three channels via the notification gateway; recipients who muted announcements in their preferences are respected.
Why it matters: maintenance notices, policy changes, and promos can now reach shops and customers directly — a capability the platform simply didn't have.

2 Referral Analytics NEW

Referrals are a core growth lever (25 RCN to the referrer, 10 to the referee), but referral data was only ever exposed customer-facing. Admins now have a platform-level view:

  • Headline stats — total / completed / pending referrals, conversion rate, and total RCN paid out, plus 7- and 30-day new-referral counts.
  • A top-referrers leaderboard (referrals, successful conversions, RCN earned).

3 Affiliate Groups Oversight NEW

RepairCoin's affiliate shop groups (coalitions that issue their own custom tokens) had zero platform oversight — every route was shop-scoped, so admins couldn't see coalition activity or, critically, the outstanding token liability. The new Affiliate Groups tab surfaces:

  • A liability summary — active/total groups, total members, and total outstanding token liability in USD.
  • A searchable list of every group with its custom token, member/holder counts, and USD liability.
  • An expandable per-group breakdown — outstanding balance, lifetime earned/redeemed, token value, and RCN allocation.
Why it matters: custom group tokens represent real financial liability. This gives the platform its first visibility into how much is outstanding across all coalitions.

4 Marketplace Analytics — Surfaced NEW

A complete Service Marketplace Analytics screen (marketplace health score + platform analytics) had been built but was never mounted anywhere. We wired it up as a Marketplace tab and fixed its outdated authentication so it works with the current login system.

5 Cleanup CLEANUP

Removed another obsolete component (an auto-complete-purchases tool that relied on a removed authentication pattern, a since-deleted endpoint, and a blockchain-era concept that no longer applies in database-only mode).
