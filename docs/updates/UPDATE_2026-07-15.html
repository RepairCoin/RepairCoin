Engineering Update

Onboarding, Referrals & Admin — Verified for Launch

Three more checklist sections traced in database-only mode: new users onboard, referrals pay out, and every admin tool loads clean.

Date:  July 15, 2026 Status:  ✓ Verified · 2 leaks fixed Focus:  Go-live checklist §2 / §5 / §7

Summary

Cleared three more go-live sections ahead of the launch. New shops and customers can register and reach their dashboards on wallet-login + profile alone — no blockchain step blocks anyone. Referral payouts (25 / 10 RCN) credit the database balance with no chain dependency. All five recently-built admin tools load without crashing and leak no blockchain content. Two cosmetic wording leaks on the shop registration form were found and fixed; a couple of behaviour notes are flagged for the launch tester.

2 Onboarding VERIFIED 2 LEAKS FIXED

Traced shop and customer registration end-to-end, frontend and backend:

  • Shop: registration gates on wallet login + profile fields only. The backend creates a plain shop record (pending admin verification) with no mint/staking/RCG step, and redirects to the dashboard. The post-registration OnboardingModal's RCG option is correctly gated off; the subscription prompt is a business gate, not a blockchain one.
  • Customer: registration completes on login + name/email, referral code optional. Pure DB write, no on-chain dependency; referral processing is wrapped so it can't fail the signup.
  • Fixed: two leftover blockchain phrasings on the shop form — the "Wallet Information" section heading (→ "Account Information"), the "0x…" reimbursement placeholder (→ "Defaults to your connected account"), and the terms line "All transactions are recorded on the blockchain" (→ "securely recorded for your records").
Flag for product (not blockchain): if a customer signs up with an invalid referral code in the link (?ref=), registration is rejected. A malformed or stale share link would block that one signup — worth confirming how referral links are generated before launch.

5 Referrals VERIFIED

  • Referrer earns 25 RCN, referee earns 10 RCN, both credited straight to the database balance via a plain SQL update — transactions are recorded balanceOnly with no blockchain call. No on-chain requirement anywhere in the path.
  • The referral dashboard reads DB stats (total / successful / pending / earned) — no wallet involved.
Timing note: payout fires on the referee's first completed repair, not at registration. Right after a referee signs up, the dashboard correctly shows the referral as pending with 0 earned; the 25 / 10 RCN land once they complete one qualifying service. The launch tester must complete a repair to see balances move.

7 Admin tools VERIFIED

Spot-checked the five recently built/fixed tools for crashes and leaks — all clean:

  • Audit Log — null-safe on action/entity/timestamp; the earlier crash on empty rows is confirmed fixed.
  • Revenue Analytics — NaN-safe formatting; Stakers/DAO split and staker projections gated off.
  • Webhook Monitor — the false "degraded" fix holds; an empty webhook table at launch reports healthy.
  • Announcements — composer renders, recipient counts null-safe.
  • Pending shops — list + Approve / Review / Reject actions all present.
Note: two internal filter dropdowns still list contract (Audit Log) and thirdweb (Webhook source) as options. They only filter historical log rows and show no blockchain data — left as-is so existing logs stay filterable.
