## 💡 Best Practices

### **Email Writing:**
- Use conversational tone (like talking to a friend)
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Include social proof (testimonials, stats)
- Single clear CTA per email
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use emojis sparingly (adds personality but don't overdo)

### **Timing:**
- Send emails Tuesday-Thursday (best open rates)
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)
- Send between 10am-2pm in shop's local timezone
- Space emails 3-4 days apart (not too aggressive)

### **Personalization:**
- Use shop name, not "Dear Customer"
- Reference their inventory size if known
- Segment by shop type/size
- Show relevant features first
- Mention local success stories when possible

### **Follow-Up:**
- Reply to every response within 24 hours
- Track who opens but doesn't click (re-engage differently)
- Celebrate early adopters publicly (with permission)
- Share quick wins and success stories weekly
