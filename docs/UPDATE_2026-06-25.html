2 Shop Approval Assistant — NEW Admin AI #3

AI screening for pending shop applications, shown in the review screen before approve/reject.

  • Checks for duplicate email / wallet / phone / business name across shops (a strong fraud signal), profile completeness, and legitimacy markers.
  • Returns a risk level, recommendation, summary, risk flags, and legitimacy signals as a clean "AI Screening" card.
  • Human-in-the-loop ("reviewer aid, not a decision"); deterministic rules fallback if AI is unavailable.
