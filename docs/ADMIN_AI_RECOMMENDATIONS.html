Admin AI — Recommendations & Roadmap

RepairCoin · Prepared June 22, 2026

Prepared by: Zeff · Focus: AI for the platform-operator role — oversight, protection, and decision support

Scope: These recommendations are for the admin / platform-operator seat specifically — protecting the token economy, monitoring the whole marketplace, and supporting platform decisions. They complement (don't overlap) the shop/customer-facing AI ideas in the separate "AI Features" doc.

◉ What Admin AI Exists Today

Current admin-side AI is mostly configuration & cost visibility — not yet decision support or protection:

  • AI settings panel — enable/disable AI per shop, set budgets & escalation thresholds.
  • Platform cost summary — aggregate AI spend, top spenders, trends.
  • Per-campaign AI cost tracking — true-margin visibility for the ads system.

Gap: there's no AI that protects the token economy, answers questions about the whole platform, or predicts/forecasts for the operator. That's where the biggest value is.

★ Recommended Admin AI Features

Priority 1

Fraud & Abuse Detection — protect the token economy

A rewards platform with a tradeable token is a target for abuse. This is the highest-stakes gap.

What it does

  • Flags suspicious reward issuance / redemption patterns — e.g., a shop issuing large RCN to a handful of wallets, rapid earn-then-redeem cycles, or shop–customer collusion.
  • Detects fake reviews, review brigading, and self-dealing across the marketplace.
  • Scores each anomaly with a severity + plain-English explanation; routes the riskiest to an admin review queue with a recommended action (freeze, investigate, dismiss).

Why it wins

  • Directly protects platform integrity and treasury — the thing most likely to cause real loss.
  • Reuses the existing anomaly-detection pipeline and emergency-freeze tooling; extends them platform-wide.
Builds on: Anomaly detector, freeze service, transaction ledger
Effort: Medium
Value: Risk reduction (highest stakes)
Priority 2

Platform Health Copilot — "Ask the platform"

An admin-scoped version of the shop insights assistant — natural-language Q&A over the entire platform.

What it does

  • Admin asks things like: "How many shops churned this month?", "Which shops are at risk of canceling?", "Platform-wide RCN issued vs. redeemed?", "Top 10 shops by revenue?"
  • Agentic tool-use over platform data → returns metrics, tables, and short narrative answers.
  • Optional daily executive briefing: an auto-generated digest of what needs attention.

Why it wins

  • Reuses the proven 19-tool insights engine — re-scope the tools from one-shop to platform-wide with new admin tools.
  • Turns scattered dashboards into one conversational surface; big time-saver for operators.
Builds on: Insights agent + tools, admin analytics
Effort: Medium
Value: Operator productivity
Priority 3

Shop Churn Prediction & Retention — protect subscription revenue

The core business is the $500/mo shop subscription. Losing shops is the #1 revenue risk.

What it does

  • Scores each shop's likelihood to cancel from usage signals (declining bookings, low AI/feature engagement, support friction, payment issues).
  • Surfaces an at-risk shops list with the "why" and a recommended intervention (outreach, discount, success check-in).
  • Pairs with revenue forecasting — projected MRR with/without intervention.

Why it wins

  • Protects recurring revenue directly; even a small churn reduction pays for itself.
  • Data already exists (subscriptions, bookings, activity) — mostly modeling + a dashboard.
Builds on: Subscription + booking + activity data
Effort: Medium
Value: Revenue protection
Priority 4

Token Economy & Treasury Advisor

Unique to RepairCoin: an AI that watches the RCN/RCG economy and advises the operator.

What it does

  • Monitors RCN issuance vs. redemption, treasury balance/runway, and RCG tier distribution over time.
  • Alerts on imbalances (e.g., issuance outpacing redemption) and recommends pricing or policy adjustments.
  • "What-if" simulations: project the effect of a reward-rate or tier-threshold change before applying it.

Why it wins

  • Nothing else gives the operator a health view of the token economy — strategic and unique.
  • Builds on the now database-backed treasury & RCG data (post blockchain-removal).
Data readiness (June 2026): the underlying numbers are now real and DB-derived, so this advisor is feasible today:
  • RCN circulating supply — computed from the transaction ledger (minted − redeemed). (was 0/chain-only before)
  • RCG circulating supply & holder count — summed from admin-managed shop balances. (was hardcoded constants)
  • RCG total supply & allocations — fixed tokenomics constants (100M, defined splits) by design; staking is N/A in database-only mode.
Builds on: Treasury repo, RCG service, transaction ledger
Effort: Medium
Value: Strategic / unique

→ Recommended Path

Start with Fraud & Abuse Detection — it's the highest-stakes gap for a token-rewards platform and reuses the anomaly + freeze tooling we already have. Platform Health Copilot is the best quick-win after that (it re-scopes the existing insights engine to platform level), and Shop Churn Prediction protects the subscription revenue that funds everything.
Suggested sequence: 1) Fraud Detection (protect) → 2) Platform Copilot (oversee) → 3) Churn Prediction (retain) → 4) Token Economy Advisor (optimize).
