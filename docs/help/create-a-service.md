# How do I create a service?

Add a new service to your shop's catalog so customers can find and book
it from the marketplace.

## When to do this

Any time you offer something new — a new repair type, class, treatment,
or package — and want customers to be able to book it directly.

## Steps

1. From the shop dashboard, open the **Services** tab in the left
   sidebar.
2. Click the yellow **+** button in the top-right of the Services tab
   header.
3. Fill in **Basic Info**:
   - **Service Name** (required, up to 100 characters) — e.g.,
     *"iPhone Screen Repair"* or *"Haircut & Style"*.
   - **Category** (required) — pick the closest match from the
     dropdown.
4. Fill in **Details**:
   - **Short Description** (optional, up to 200 characters) — one to
     three lines explaining what's included.
   - **Price (USD)** (required) — must be greater than 0.
5. Optional: upload a **Service Photo** in the **Visuals** section.
   Good photos drive more bookings.
6. Optional: add up to 5 **Tags** in the **Discovery** section. Tags
   help customers find your service via search.
7. The **Active Status** toggle in **Status** defaults to ON. Leave it
   on if you want the service visible in the marketplace immediately,
   or flip it off to save the service as inactive.
8. Optional: configure the **AI Sales Assistant** section at the
   bottom of the form — see
   [configure-the-ai-sales-agent.md](configure-the-ai-sales-agent.md).
9. Click **Create Service** at the bottom of the form.

You'll be redirected back to the Services tab and the new service will
appear in the grid.

## Common pitfalls

- **The + button is disabled or greyed out.** Your shop needs either an
  active subscription or 10,000+ RCG to create services. Hover the
  disabled button to see the exact reason, then activate your
  subscription or top up RCG.
- **Duration isn't on the create form.** Service duration is set
  separately on the **Availability** tab of each service so customers
  can book the right time slot — see
  [set-appointment-hours.md](set-appointment-hours.md).
- **New service doesn't show up in the marketplace.** Check the
  service's **Active** status. Inactive services are hidden from
  customers but still visible to you in the Services tab.
