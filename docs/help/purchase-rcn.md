# How do I purchase RCN for my shop?

Top up the RCN token balance your shop uses to issue rewards to
customers. RCN is priced at $0.10 each, with tiered discounts based on
your shop's RCG holdings.

## When to do this

Before you run out — issuing a reward to a customer fails if your RCN
balance is too low. Most shops top up monthly or after a busy week.

## Steps

1. From the shop dashboard, open the **Purchase** tab in the left
   sidebar.
2. Enter the amount of RCN you want to purchase (in tokens), or the
   USD amount you want to spend — the form converts between the two.
3. Review the tiered price shown:
   - **Standard tier** — $0.10 per RCN.
   - **Premium / Elite tiers** — discounted per-RCN price based on
     your shop's RCG holdings.
4. Complete the purchase via the checkout flow.
5. Once payment clears, RCN is credited to your shop's available
   balance and you can immediately issue rewards from it.

You can review past purchases in the **Purchase History** section on
the same tab.

## Common pitfalls

- **Purchase tab is disabled.** Your shop needs an active subscription
  or 10,000+ RCG to purchase RCN. See
  [subscription-and-billing.md](subscription-and-billing.md).
- **Cancelled subscription, still need history.** The Purchase History
  section stays accessible even if your subscription lapses, so you
  can pull past invoices and records. New purchases require an active
  status.
- **Tier discount didn't apply.** Tier qualification is based on
  current RCG balance at the time of purchase. If you topped up RCG
  right before purchasing RCN, your tier may not have refreshed yet —
  give it a moment or refresh the page.
