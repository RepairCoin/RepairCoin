# How do I configure the AI Sales Agent for a service?

Set up the AI assistant that replies to customers in chat for a
specific service — tone, behavior, and FAQ answers.

## When to do this

After creating a service, when you want the AI to handle initial
customer questions instead of you replying manually. Each service has
its own AI configuration.

## Steps

1. Open the service detail page (**Services** tab → click the service).
2. Click the **AI Assistant** tab at the top of the service detail
   page. (A small green dot on the tab label means AI is already
   enabled for this service.)
3. Configure the section labelled **Auto Sales & Booking**:
   - **Master toggle** (top-right of the section) — turn AI replies
     on or off for this service. Off means a human handles every chat.
   - **Tone** — pick how the AI talks to customers: **Friendly**,
     **Professional**, or **Urgent**. Click **See How the AI Replies**
     to preview a sample message in your chosen tone.
   - **Suggest upsells** — when checked, the AI mentions related
     services in conversation when relevant.
   - **Enable booking assistance** — when checked, the AI can propose
     tap-to-book slots when customers ask about availability.
4. Optional: fill in **FAQ entries** below — see
   [manage-faqs.md](manage-faqs.md).
5. Click **Save Changes** in the sticky bar at the bottom of the tab.

The "Unsaved changes" hint next to the Save button confirms you have
pending edits.

## Common pitfalls

- **AI is enabled but customers still get human-only replies.** The
  AI Sales Agent capability is also admin-gated at the shop level. If
  your service-level toggle is on but you see no AI replies, contact
  RepairCoin — your shop's master AI switch may not be flipped on yet.
- **Tone doesn't match what you expected.** Use **See How the AI
  Replies** to preview before saving. The preview shows the same
  reply Claude would produce for a sample customer question.
- **Edits lost when switching tabs.** Switching to another tab with
  unsaved changes triggers a confirm dialog — click Cancel to return
  and Save first.
